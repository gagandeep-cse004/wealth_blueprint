Claim Process Information

Here's what to expect after submitting your claim:

  • You'll receive a confirmation email with your claim reference number
  • Our claims team will review your submission within 48 hours
  • You may be contacted for additional information or documentation
  • Once approved, payment will be processed within 7-10 business days
  • You can track your claim status using the reference number

Required Documents

Depending on your claim type, you may need to provide:

  • Policy document or policy number
  • Identity proof (Aadhar, PAN, etc.)
  • Medical reports and bills (for health claims)
  • Police FIR (for accident or theft claims)
  • Photographs of damage (for property claims)
  • Death certificate (for life insurance claims)
