Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our investment plans

What types of investments do you offer ?

We offer a range of investment options: low-risk bonds, balanced equity-debt portfolios, and high-growth strategies.

How do I know which investment plan is right for me?

Schedule a free consultation, and our advisors will recommend the best plan based on your financial goals and risk profile.

Can I make additional contributions?

Yes, you can make additional contributions anytime. Flexible monthly contributions start from ₹5,000.

How can I track my investment ?

You can track your portfolio performance through our online portal or mobile app with real-time updates and detailed reports.

Is my investment secure?

Yes, your investment is secure. Wealth Blue Print is a SEBI registered investment advisor. We follow strict regulatory guidelines to ensure the safety of your investments. All transactions are secure and transparent. Additionally, we implement robust risk management strategies tailored to each investment plan.

What are the tax implications of these investments?

Tax implications depend on the type of assets in your portfolio and holding period. Generally, equity investments held for more than one year are subject to long-term capital gains tax at 10% (above ₹1 lakh). Debt investments are taxed based on your income tax slab if held for less than three years, and at 20% with indexation benefits if held for more than three years. Our tax advisors can provide personalized guidance based on your portfolio.
