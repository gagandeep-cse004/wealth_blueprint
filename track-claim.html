Claim Processing Information

Here's what to expect during the claim processing:

  • Document verification typically takes 1-2 business days
  • Claim assessment may take 3-5 business days depending on complexity
  • Final approval is usually completed within 1-2 business days after assessment
  • Payment processing takes 1-3 business days after approval
  • Total processing time is usually 7-10 business days for standard claims
